Every time you place a Resource on your Network is called an [Instance]( https://docs.artemisplatform.com/needtoknowterms) of that Resource. There can be many Instances of the same Resource.

The walkthrough below covers the process of creating an Instance. The following two pages of documentation cover how you connect new Instances to your Network and enhance/navigate the documentation of each Instance.
