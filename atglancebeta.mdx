Welcome to Artemis! We offer a platform to document and diagram data science projects. The walkthrough below showcases our core capabilities and introduces you to our interface. If this piques your interest but you're not yet an Artemis user please visit our [landing page](https://www.artemisplatform.com/) and get in touch!

### Primary Capabilities
<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Resource Catalog" icon="grid-2" color="#ea5a0c" href="https://docs.artemisplatform.com/betaresourcecatalog">
    Document every database, script, model brought to bear on a given project in one place. 
  </Card>
  <Card title="Networks" icon="chart-network" color="#0285c7" href="https://docs.artemisplatform.com/betanetworks">
    Networks let you diagram how different resources relate. They're a great way to visualize your projects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pipelines" icon="timeline" color="#16a34a" href="/components/card-group">
    Artemis lets you visualize how your pipeliens are laid out and capture the different parameters used each time they're run.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Views" icon="table" color="#dc2626" href="/components/card-group">
    Compare parameters side by side between different resources and different instances of the same resource.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
